Simone Mutti

Simone Mutti is a post-doctoral researcher at Università degli Studi di Bergamo. His research interests are in the area of OS security, privacy, and data management, with particular consideration of access control in emerging scenario. From August 2014 to November 2014, he spent 3 months as a Visiting PhD in the Computer Security Group at University of California - Santa Barbara, under the supervision of professors Christopher Kruegel and Giovanni Vigna where he contributed to the implementation of a new infrastructure for the detection of Android malware.
